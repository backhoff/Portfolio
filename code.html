Code of Ethics

  1. Avoid harm to others.
  2. Be honest and trustworthy.
  3. Be fair, give credit.
  4. Respect the privacy of others.
  5. Acquire and maintain professional competence.
  6. Know and respect the laws pertaining to professional work.
  7. Accept full responsibility for my work.
  8. Approve software, only if it is safe, passes tests, and meets specifications.
